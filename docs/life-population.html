Life Expectancy and Population

The relationship between the size of a country’s population and the average life expectancy of its citizens.

Research Question: We want to see if larger countries tend to have higher or lower life expectancy compared to smaller nations.

Code 
import pandas as pd
import plotly.express as px

df = pd.read_csv('gapminder.csv')

fig = px.scatter(df, 
                 x="population_millions", 
                 y="life_expectancy",
                 color="continent",
                 hover_name="country",
                 title="Relationship Between Population and Life Expectancy",
                 labels={
                     "population_millions": "Population (Millions)",
                     "life_expectancy": "Life Expectancy (Years)"
                 })

fig.update_layout(xaxis_type="log")
fig.show()

A slight positive correlation between population and life expectancy in all continents.
